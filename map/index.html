About Half Moon Rock

Half Moon is a rock outcropping located in Red River Gorge. To reach the beautiful view you must hike an unmarked trail about .8 miles long. Then scale the side of the rock face. This is not for the faint of heart. Half Moon rock initially attracted repellers who would repel down the cliffs on each side. After many years of wear and tear on the trees they eventually died. This cleared the top and allowed for a better 360 view from the top. No longer is Half Moon Rock know for its repelling but for its amazing view.

Tools used in this project

  • ArcGIS Pro was used to process lidar data and produce elevation derivatives.
  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled.
